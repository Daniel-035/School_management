## What Was Built
A Flutter mobile app for school staff (teachers, class coordinators, operational staff) implementing the full PRD across 6 features:

1. **Dashboard** — Greeting, today's schedule, pending tasks, recent announcements
2. **Attendance Management** — Daily/Subject-wise mode, P/A/L segmented control, summary, Monthly Report
3. **Homework & Assignments** — List with due-date chips, New Assignment bottom sheet (title, description, subject, class, due date, attachments)
4. **Examination & Grading** — Exam selector, marks entry, class performance bar chart (fl_chart), Average/Highest/Pass-% metrics
5. **Communication** — Noticeboard posts + Direct Messages (with admin-oversight banner, Pending/Approved status)
6. **Timetable Viewer** — Week-day chips, daily schedule list (reachable from dashboard)

## Architecture
- **Pattern:** Feature-first clean architecture with `core/`, `data/`, `features/`
- **State:** `provider` (`ChangeNotifier` `SchoolRepository` in-memory with seeded data)
- **Routing:** `go_router` with `StatefulShellRoute.indexedStack` (state-preserving tabs)
- **UI:** Material 3, custom green theme
- **Libs:** `intl`, `uuid`, `fl_chart`, `table_calendar`, `flutter_lints`
